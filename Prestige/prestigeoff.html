  • STRENGTHS

    • #1 and only local beer brand in Haiti • Premium quality lager beer winner of 2 gold medals • High brand awareness and top of mind • -/+ 90 market share • In Haiti beer = Prestige • Haiti is proud of its beer • Part of the HNK portfolio

  • WEAKNESSES

    • Limited brand support in recent past • Very low visibility and share of voice • Lack of brand experience • Only available in 2 SKUs • Not very attractive packaging • Not innovative

  • OPPORTUNITIES

    Line extensions, new pack types • Population of 10 million with 50% 25 • Prestige Export: Important Haitian community in FL, NY, Canada, DR and France • Government is promoting tourism • Better road conditions in countryside, better distribution

  • THREATS

    • New local beer or import beer with huge marketing budget trying to take shares from Prestige • Entry of new beer priced lower than Prestige • Increase of government taxation on alcohol • Contraband (cheap rum) from the DR

    defines the who, why and when we target with our brand.     is a compelling statement that captures the heart of the target consumer’s motivations. The consumer insight needs to be unlocked in order to make the brand relevant to the consumer. The key insight for Prestige is: – Live in Haiti is challenging – Belonging and Moving forward in this life requires hard work – But also time to relax together with friends
